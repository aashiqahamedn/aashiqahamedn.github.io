Encode message

1. Select the image you want to use for encoding the message.
2. Enter the text you wish to hide within the image.
3. Click the Encode button to embed the message into the image.
4. Save the newly generated image, as it will contain the hidden message.
5. Keep in mind that larger messages require larger images. If the selected image is too small to hold the message, you will receive a notification.

Privacy Assurance – The entire process happens within your browser; neither the image nor the hidden message is transmitted over the web.
